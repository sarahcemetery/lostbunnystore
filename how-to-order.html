How to Order

Ordering from Lost Bunny Store is easy! Here's how:

  1. Browse our products: Explore our collection of handmade creations.
  2. Customize your item: Click on the heart icon next to the product you're interested in. This will take you to a customization form where you can select your preferred options.
  3. Add to your shopping bag: Once you've customized your item, click "I Like It" to add it to your shopping bag.
  4. Review your order: Visit the shopping bag to review all the items you've added.
  5. Save your order: Click "Save as Image" to save a picture of your order summary.
  6. Contact me: Send the saved image of your order to me via Telegram at @lostbunnyhelp.

Please note that all items are handmade and may take some time to create.
